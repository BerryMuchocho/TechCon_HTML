History

TechCon began as a small gathering of developers and engineers who wanted a space to share practical insights and new ideas. Over the years it has grown into an international conference featuring innovation, collaboration and hands-on learning.

Mission

Our mission is to bring together people who are passionate about building and improving technology. TechCon focuses on real problems, practical solutions and pushing boundaries in software, hardware and everything in between.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ellison is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and responsible machine learning. She has published widely and advises global companies on ethical technology development.
